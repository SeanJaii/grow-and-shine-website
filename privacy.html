Privacy

Grow and Shine Childminding respects your privacy. This notice explains how we handle information sent through this website, by email, or by phone.

Information we collect

When you make an enquiry, we may receive your name, contact details, your child's age, requested start date, and any information you choose to share about your childcare needs.

How we use information

We use enquiry information to respond to you, discuss availability, arrange visits, and provide childcare information. We do not sell your information.

Third-party services

This website uses Google Fonts and embeds Google Maps. Those services may receive technical information such as your IP address and browser details when the page loads.

Retention

We keep enquiry information only for as long as needed to respond to your request, manage availability, or meet legal and safeguarding obligations.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to Lanzanal@aol.com.
